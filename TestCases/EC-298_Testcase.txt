Positive Test Cases:
1. Verify that the login page displays Username, Password fields, and a Login button when navigating to https://www.saucedemo.com/
2. Verify successful login with valid credentials ('standard_user', 'sauce') and redirection to /inventory.html

1. Verify error message displayed for invalid credentials (e.g., 'invalid_user', 'wrong_password')

1. Verify login with maximum character limit in Username and Password fields.
2. Verify login with single character for Username and Password.

Edge Test Cases:

2. Verify that the application handles slow network conditions during login gracefully.
